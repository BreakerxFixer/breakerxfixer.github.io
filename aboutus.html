We are BreakerxFixer, a small team focused on both offensive and defensive security, CTF challenges, and hands-on vulnerability research.
Our work revolves around breaking systems, hardening them, understanding how they fail, protecting them, and documenting the process through detailed writeups and Proofs of Concept.
We take a practical approach to cybersecurity — no empty theory, no recycled content — everything we share is built, tested, and understood from the ground up.
From exploiting vulnerabilities to analyzing defensive mechanisms, our goal is to cover the full attack surface and develop a deeper understanding of real-world security scenarios.
We are constantly learning, building, and refining our skills, aiming to contribute meaningful insights to the community while evolving as security researchers.
