ZERO-PII LOGGING POLICY

1. Introduction

Welcome to BREAKER_X_FIXER. Our mission is to provide an elite, educational cybersecurity playground while remaining completely anonymous. We operate under a strict philosophy: we cannot lose what we do not collect.

2. Anonymous Authentication System

We do NOT ask for your real name. We do NOT ask for your email. We do NOT require any personally identifiable information to create an account.

  • You register using only a username (alias) and a password of your choice.
  • Your password is NEVER stored in plain text. It is hashed using industry-standard bcrypt before being saved. Neither the platform nor its administrators can read your password.
  • Your account and all earned progress live on our servers. Clearing your browser cache or switching devices does NOT delete your account — you can always log back in with your username and password.
  • If you forget your password, there is no recovery mechanism (since we hold no contact information). Keep your credentials safe.

3. Data Stored On Our Servers

The only data points stored in our databases are:

  • Username — e.g., hacker_99 — your chosen alias, never a real name
  • Password hash — a bcrypt hash of your password. The original password is mathematically unrecoverable from this hash
  • CTF Score — numeric points accumulated by solving challenges
  • Solve timestamps — time of each solved challenge
  • Avatar image — optional profile picture uploaded by you, stored in our media bucket

We do not store IP addresses, device fingerprints, geolocation data, email addresses, real names, or any other form of personally identifiable information.

4. Analytics & Tracking

We run zero tracking scripts. No Google Analytics. No Meta Pixels. No third-party data brokers. Our web servers generate standard access logs for security diagnostics only, which are rotated and purged routinely.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time by clicking “Terminate session” → “Delete entity data” in the session panel. This action is irreversible.

6. Educational Purposes Only

Everything within this domain is provided strictly for educational purposes and authorized penetration testing practice. Any malicious use of techniques or knowledge acquired here against systems you do not own or have explicit permission to test is strictly prohibited.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at:
