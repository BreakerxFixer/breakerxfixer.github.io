ZERO-PII LOGGING POLICY

1. Introduction

Welcome to BREAKER_X_FIXER. Our mission is to provide an elite, educational cybersecurity playground while remaining completely anonymous. We operate under a strict philosophy: we cannot lose what we do not collect.

2. Anonymous Authentication System

We do NOT ask for emails. We do NOT ask for passwords. We do NOT ask for your real name.

  • You register using a single, unique 'Username'.
  • The system derives an irreversible signature stored offline in your browser's local storage.
  • If you clear your cache, your progress is permanently lost. There is no password recovery because there are no passwords.

3. Data Retention

The only data points existing on our databases are:

  • Username (e.g., hacker_99)
  • CTF Score (Numeric points value)
  • Timestamp (Time of last solved challenge)

4. Analytics & Tracking

We use absolute zero tracking scripts. No Google Analytics. No Meta Pixels. No third-party data handlers. Our servers generate standard access logs for security diagnostics which are rotated and pruned routinely.

5. Educational Purposes Only

Everything within this domain is provided strictly for educational purposes and authorized penetration testing practice. Any malicious use of knowledge acquired here against external systems goes completely against our terms of existence.
